About Us

Jabalpur, formerly known as Jubbulpore, is a vibrant city located on the banks of the Narmada River in the state of Madhya Pradesh, India. Jabalpur was selected as one of the first 7 smart cities in India under the Smart Cities Mission, aiming to improve infrastructure and quality of life for its residents.

Jabalpur is a city rich in culture and tradition, reflecting a blend of various influences due to its historical significance and diverse population.

  • Festivals: The people of Jabalpur celebrate a wide range of festivals with great enthusiasm. Major festivals include Diwali, Holi, Navratri, and Dussehra. The city also celebrates local festivals like Kartik Mela and Narmada Jayanti, which are unique to the region.
  • Art and Craft: Jabalpur is known for its traditional handicrafts, including terracotta, wooden toys, and stone carvings. The city has a vibrant artisan community that keeps these age-old crafts alive.
  • Cuisine: The culinary traditions of Jabalpur are a mix of North Indian and Central Indian flavors. Popular dishes include poha, jalebi, samosa, and a variety of street foods. The city is also known for its sweets and traditional snacks.
